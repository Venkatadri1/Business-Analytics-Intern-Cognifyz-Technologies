Mutual Funds Data Analysis – Business Analytics Internship
🏢 About the Internship

This project was completed as part of the Business Analytics Internship Program at Cognifyz Technologies, a company focused on delivering innovative solutions in data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and analytics.

The internship provided hands-on experience in analyzing real-world datasets and applying analytical techniques to generate meaningful business insights.

📌 Project Overview

The Mutual Funds Data Analysis project focuses on understanding investor behavior, financial preferences, and decision-making patterns using a structured dataset.

The analysis aims to identify trends, patterns, and key factors influencing investment decisions.

🎯 Objectives

Understand the structure of the dataset

Analyze investor demographics (age, gender)

Identify most preferred investment avenues

Study reasons behind investment choices

Explore savings objectives of investors

Analyze investment duration and expectations

Identify common sources of financial information

Perform correlation analysis between key variables
